• What is Real-Time Bidding?

• Real-time bidding (RTB)is a technology designed for automatic buying and selling of ad impressions to the highest bid in real time. It functions on the basis of online auctions and works according to the per-impression (CPM) payment model. The whole process is getting powered by programmatic platforms, each of which represents the interests of the media-trading participants: the supply side (SSP) and the demand side (DSP).
